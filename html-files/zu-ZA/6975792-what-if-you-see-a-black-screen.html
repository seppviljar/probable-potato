What if you see a black screen?

There are several reasons why you might see black screen instead of a camera stream. Please find a platform you are using below or check the general black screen issues section.

General

  • The most common reason for seeing a black screen is that some other service is using your device's camera. The camera's video service can not be used by multiple applications simultaneously. Please make sure to close other applications that might be using your camera. The same applies to applications that recently used your camera, but are still active and running. Some common applications include Skype, FaceTime, Messenger or any live streaming services. After closing the other applications, please refresh the page or restart the device and try again.

  • It might be that your camera's resolution is too low to get a good quality pictures and our system is automatically blocking the camera. The minimum requirement is 480px x 640px. If your camera's resolution is lower that this, it's not possible to proceed and we recommend using a different device.

  • A black screen instead of the camera stream is also shown when the browser used is not supported. You can check if you are using a supported browser here.

  • It's possible that your network does not allow you to share your camera stream. To eliminate this issue, please connect to a different network and try again.

Android

The problem is most likely that our service has not been given a permission to use your web camera and microphone. Here's how to do it:

1. Tap the lock-shaped button

2. Tap "Site settings"

3. Tap on camera and microphone permissions and choose "allow"

4. Click back to resume to the verification process, refresh the page and try again

iOS (Apple)

The problem is most likely that our service has not been given a permission to use your web camera and microphone. Here's how to do it:

  1. Tap refresh button

2. Allow our service to use your web camera and microphone

3. Sometimes you also need to click on the disallowed camera button and refresh the page after doing so
